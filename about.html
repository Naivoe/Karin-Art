Welcome to Karin's art studio

Free-your-mind

My name is Karin and I live in a city close to Amsterdam, in the northern part of The Netherlands. I flew over 35 years as a flight attendant for KLM. However, now I am a pensionada, this naturely comes with free time.

All paintings are made by myself. Each painting reflects a feeling, a phase of life or simply a moment at that time.

I see myself as an free artist and I like to do everything in my own way. I do not want to go to classes, because who determines what you want to paint and which brush you'll have to use? As a free painter I determine myself.

I love colours and use them in every way

I hope you like my paintings. If you're interested in one or more of my paintings, please go to the contact page and send me an E-mail.
